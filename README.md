Alva Sorcerer
==============
Sorcerer is the only 3D stage animation suite. It authors Animated Lighting, Video & Audio (ALVA) content for existing Front of House lighting consoles and sound playbacks.

<img src="/images/alva_sorcerer_fcurves.png" alt="Hero" width="500">

# Compatibility
#### Sorcerer authors ALVA content for:
- ETC Eos: Full (Lighting)
- grandMA3: Partial (Lighting)
- QLab: Full (Audio)

#### Sorcerer is a Blender add-on. Blender is ["the free and open source 3D creation suite."](https://www.blender.org/) 
- Currently developed on Blender 4.4.1
- Generally compatible down to Blender 4.0
- Testing on Blender 5 coming soon

About
-------
Sorcerer is developed and maintained by Alva Theaters, whose mission it is to mass-produce enhanced live performances with a network of 100 identical theaters across America. Development on Sorcerer's predecessor, Alva Sequencer, began in summer 2023. Work on Sorcerer began in winter 2023. Sequencer remains free and open source, but is not currently active. Sorcerer is currently in the early stages of an early access program for real world testing.
